About Us

Our business, InkHub, operates as a sole proprietor, on demand printing services to individuals, students and so on. Located at Taman Lembah Bujang, Merbok, Kedah which are strategic locations near universities and residents. We provide services like binding, laminating, printing, and photostat. We use the latest and high-quality digital printing technology to ensure top notch results are delivered quickly and satisfactorily for customers. The business was started by our observations about a high demand for fast, reliable and late-hour printing services, especially for students and professional workers that are in crisis for printing. All the printing outside there only operates withing limited hours, that is why our InkHub established a great solution for those problems for all users. This business plan has been mentioned about our outline operational plan, details about our business structure for marketing and financial plan to make our business successful with a great result by following up our vision and mission to ensure our business goes as a plan.
